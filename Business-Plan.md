# [Personal] AI Supplement Analyzer: Business Plan v1.1

## 1. Value Proposition
A lightweight web tool that helps health-conscious users verify the compatibility and effectiveness of their supplement stack using advanced AI.

## 2. Target Audience
- Biohackers and fitness enthusiasts.
- Elderly individuals managing multiple nutrients.
- People newly starting a health regimen.

## 3. Product Roadmap (The "Vibe" Path)
- **V1 (MVP):** Simple input form -> AI compatibility check -> Result cards. [DONE]
- **V2:** Local Caching & Feedback Loop (Like/Verified). [DONE]
- **V3:** Coupang Partners Integration (Top-rated product recommendations). [PLANNED]
- **V4:** Supabase integration -> User accounts -> "My Stack" history.

## 4. Monetization Strategy (Passive Income)
- **Affiliate (Coupang Partners):** Automatically link to Top 2 highly-rated products on Coupang for every search term (e.g., "Recommended Magnesium").
- **Referrals:** "Best Price" buttons linking to iHerb for international users.
- **Freemium:** 3 analyses/day free, unlimited for $5/month.

## 5. Marketing (The Distribution)
- Post helpful compatibility charts on r/supplements.
- Create a "Supplement Stack Checker" widget for health blogs.
- SEO focused on "Is it safe to take [X] and [Y] together?".
